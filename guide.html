Auto Next Page Reader — Important setup

This extension reads article content aloud and tries to navigate to the next chapter automatically.

Tips
  • Use Ctrl + ` to pause/resume while a page is being read.
  • If auto-next fails, try sites that use a rel="next" link or URLs with chapter-123 patterns.
  • Keep the tab active while reading for the most reliable speech output.

Buttons

  • Start Reading — extracts the main content on the current page and starts text-to-speech.
  • Stop — stops the current speech immediately.
  • Skip Forward — jumps ahead in the current article.
  • Auto Next — when ON, after finishing a chapter the extension tries to open the next one.
  • Auto Read — when ON, it starts reading automatically after a page loads.

Privacy & permissions

The extension runs its content script on pages you visit to extract readable text and control speech. No data is sent off your device.
